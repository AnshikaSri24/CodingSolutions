Input Format - "[1, 2, 1, 1, 1] | [2, 2, 2, 3, 3] | [5, 2, 6, 1, 4]" if 3 players are playing the game

Output Format - "Winner is player 1  - 4 of a kind"

5 of a kind (examples)
3 3 3 3 3
2 2 2 2 2

4 of a kind (examples)
1 1 4 1 1
2 2 2 2 1

Full House (examples)
2 3 2 3 2
1 1 1 3 3

Straight (examples)
1 2 3 4 5
2 3 4 5 6
 
2 Pair (examples)
1 1 3 4 4
1 3 1 4 3

Highest Score = Sum of all scores
